

## Mecca Destinations — Luxury Villa Rental Website

A recreation of the Mecca Destinations Cabo villa rental website with matching design, layout, and feel. The site uses a warm, earthy color palette (olive green navbar, cream/beige backgrounds) with elegant typography.

---

### 🏠 Page 1: Home Page

**Navigation Bar**
- Olive green sticky navbar with the "M" logo on the left
- Links: Home, Rooms, In-House Dining
- "Book Now" button with rounded outline style on the right

**Hero Section**
- Full-width background image of Cabo's iconic rock formations
- Large centered headline: "Escape to Luxury" + "Experience Cabo Like Never Before"
- "Book Your Stay" button that scrolls to the contact/booking form

**"Unwind in the Heart of Cabo" Section**
- Two-column layout with descriptive text on the left and villa interior photo on the right
- "Book Your Stay" CTA button

**"Sleeping In or Staying Up Late" Section**
- Full-width background image with text overlay and CTA

**"Indulge in Elevated Comfort" — Features Grid**
- Four feature cards with images and short descriptions:
  - Poolside Escape
  - Close to Downtown
  - Private Beach
  - Private Chef

**Photo Gallery**
- Scrollable/carousel gallery showcasing villa photos

**Contact Booking Form**
- Functional form collecting: name, email, check-in/check-out dates, number of guests, and message
- Displays a success toast on submission (no backend needed — just front-end confirmation)

**Footer**
- "Join the Conversation" section with Instagram link
- Simple footer with branding

### 🎨 Design System
- **Primary color:** Olive green (#5C6B2F-ish) for navbar and accents
- **Background:** Warm cream/beige
- **Typography:** Clean, modern serif or sans-serif for headings, elegant feel
- **Rounded elements:** Buttons and cards with generous border radius
- **Image-heavy:** Large hero images, photo carousels throughout
- **Responsive:** Fully mobile-friendly layout
